      Frontier annual pass

Updated on Nov 21, 2022 by Admin.

There are a limited number of first-come, first-served spots available. GoWild! passes will be available at the introductory price of $599 per person until November 16.

The Frontier GoWild! Pass is valid for a year beginning May 2, 2023.

Travelers who purchase the annual pass can log into their Frontier Miles account, search and book flights up to a day before departure, and pay only $0.01 in airfare plus applicable taxes, fees, and charges.

Bags, seats, and other products are available at an additional cost.




"The GoWild! Pass is a terrific opportunity for people with flexible schedules who can hop on a flight on short notice and take advantage of the huge number of airplane seats that go empty each year," Frontierpresident and CEO Barry Biffle saidin a press release. "With the freedom and flexibility of the GoWild! All-You-Can-Fly Pass, we invite our pass holders to explore new cities, visit friends and family, and fly from snow to sun and back as often as they like."

The pass does come with some restrictions, including blackout dates in 2023 and 2024.
