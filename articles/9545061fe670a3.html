      Nancy pelosi speech

Updated on Nov 21, 2022 by Admin.

Speaker Nancy Pelosi announced on Thursday on the House floor that she will not seek a leadership position in the next Congress. The New York Times recorded her remarks, which are reproduced below. NANCY PELOSI:

Thank you very much, Madam Speaker. Madam Speaker, we are gathered here on holy ground: the United States House of Representatives, the beating heart of American democracy. I'll never forget my first sighting of the Capitol. When I was six years old, it was a cold January day. My father, Thomas D'Alesandro Jr., was about to be sworn in as a member of Congress for the fifth time, representing our beloved hometown of Baltimore.




I was in the car with my brothers, who were ecstatic, jumping up and down and telling me, "Nancy, look, there's the Capitol." And I keep saying, "I don't see any capital.

"
