      Texas governor immigration

Updated on Nov 21, 2022 by Admin.

Governor Greg Abbott today sent a letter to President Joe Biden highlighting the unprecedented level of illegal immigration at America's southern border as a result of the President's persistent failure to enforce the nation's immigration laws. Prior to President Biden's election, America had some of the lowest levels of illegal immigration in decades; however, over the last year and a half, that number has skyrocketed to more than 2 million immigrants attempting to enter the country illegally. The Governor points out that the federal government's failure to enforce America's immigration laws has forced Texas to defend its own territory against invasion by Mexican drug cartels and mass illegal immigration.

"You must reinstate the policies you eliminated, or craft and implement new policies, to fulfill your constitutional duty to enforce federal immigration laws and protect the states from invasion," the letter states. "Your two years of inaction have now forced Texas to step up our efforts to secure our state. Your open-border policies, which have triggered an unprecedented surge in illegal immigration, are the sole reason Texas has been forced to use its constitutional authority to defend itself."

Governor Abbott also directed the Texas Department of Public Safety (DPS) and Texas Military Department (TMD) to step up unprecedented efforts to secure the Texas-Mexico border earlier today. The Governor outlined the need for Operation Lone Star partners to defend Texas against what amounts to an invasion of America's southern border in a letter to DPS Director Steve McCraw and TMD Adjutant General Thomas Suelzer. Today's letter follows a letter sent to Texas county judges on Monday explaining his Executive Order, which invokes the Invasion Clauses of the United States and Texas Constitutions to fully authorize the unprecedented actions Texas is taking to secure the border in the face of an all-time high for illegal immigration.
