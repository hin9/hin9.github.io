      90 closed buffalo

Updated on Nov 21, 2022 by Admin.

While some closures that were in place this morning have been lifted along Interstate 90, eastbound I-90 remains closed as of 12:50 p.m. Thursday, according to the Wyoming Department of Transportation.

According to WYDOT, snow is falling in some areas along I-80 in southeast Wyoming, and black ice advisories are in effect in the Cheyenne area.

Other travel advisories are in effect throughout the state.




US Highway 87 eastbound is closed from Buffalo to I-90 Exit 44.

Chain Law Level 1 is in effect on Wyoming Highway 28 in the South Pass area. WYDOT provides real-time road condition information for highways throughout the state.
