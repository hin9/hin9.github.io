      Level 3 snow emergency

Updated on Nov 21, 2022 by Admin.

A level 3 snow emergency declared in Ashtabula County around 6:30 a.m. Thursday due to lake effect snow has been reduced to a level 1.

"A level 1 snow emergency means roadways are hazardous with blowing and drifting snow," according to an update from the sheriff's office at 12:45 p.m. "Roads could also be icy. Drivers are urged to exercise extreme caution."

Despite the reduction in the snow emergency level, Sheriff William Niemi urged drivers to avoid area roads until further notice.

In a statement, Sheriff Niemi said, "The roads are still bad." "We are requesting that people avoid these trouble spots and travel with extreme caution."




Sheriff Niemi had previously modified the county-wide level 3 snow emergency at 7:45 a.m. to only affect areas north of Interstate 90.
