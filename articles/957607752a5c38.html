Make sure you get off to a good start in Paldea by selecting the best starter Pokemon in Scarlet and Violet!

The latest Pokemon game has arrived, with players entering the Spain-inspired region of Paldea. You can also explore the world at your own pace for the first time in the mainline series!

However, the first decision any aspiring Pokemon Trainer must make is which starter to use. Which of the three fantastic Pokemon should you choose?

We believe Fuecoco is the best starter in Scarlet Violet because it provides the easiest start to the game due to the early Gyms.

The first three Gyms in the game, according to leaks, are Bug, Grass, and Electric-type. As a result, Fuecoco is the best choice for dealing with these early-game Gyms.

As for the final evolution of each starter, Fuecoco becomes Skeledirge, a Fire/Ghost-type.

Spirigatito evolves into Meowscarada, a Pokemon of the Grass/Dark type.




Eventually, Quaxly evolves into Quaquaval, a Water/Fighting-type Pokmon.
