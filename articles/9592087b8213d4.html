Erie County Executive Mark Poloncarz declared a state of emergency and issued a travel ban.

The State of Emergency is in effect as of 8 p.m. Thursday and the travel ban for all of Erie County will be in effect at 9 p.m. Thursday until further notice.

Poloncarz stated that the ban was implemented after consulting with local and state officials, as well as the National Weather Service. The ban's scope and locations will be reconsidered on Friday morning.

A travel advisory was issued at 7 p.m. Thursday, following Gov. Kathy Hochul's declaration of a state of emergency.
